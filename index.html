Mouse

My mouse is a Gaming mouse. It is from Logitech. The model number is G503. It perfectly fits in my hand. It has 6 side buttons for gaming. It has a "G" written on top of it, that has breathing spectrum of colors.

Keyboard

I have a gaming keyboard. It is from razer. It is a Blackwidow Chroma version. It has a perfect size. It's led options are fantastic. It has it's own app dedicated to colors. You can make 16.8 million combinations of colors. It does not have a handrest. It has razer's own designed switches. They are as loud as cherry blue switches.

MousePad

I somehow have a "gaming" mousepad. It's from razer as well. It is the razer firefly, It also has a few RGB options. In my opinion, the best one is the rainbow wave. If you do not have interest in colors, DO NOT buy this. It does not cover the keyboard, But is still pretty big.

PC

Ah, the main component of the whole setup. It is a HP OMEN. It has Nvidia Geforce 1070 ti graphics card. 16 GB DDR. 256 SSD. i7-7700k. It runs really smooth and is very fast. Never crashes. Has led lights on it. It has a clean and sleek design.

Headphones

I have a JBL headphones. It is not used for gaming. But it still is great for gaming. It has a feature that enables us the hear less from the outside world.

Monitor

I have a samsung monitor. It has a pretty fast response time. It has great size. It has 60 hz. it also has a eye saver, which is basically blue light filter.
